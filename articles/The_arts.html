The arts

The creative arts encompass an extraordinary range of human practices that express ideas, emotions, and cultural values, from the earliest known cave paintings in France’s Lascaux—estimated to be over 17,000 years old—to today’s digital installations and immersive experiences. Throughout history, humans have used art not only to document their lives but also to experiment with new forms of expression, as seen in the evolution from the precision of ancient Egyptian hieroglyphs and Greek sculpture to the innovative techniques of the Renaissance, where figures like Leonardo da Vinci and Michelangelo redefined the possibilities of perspective, anatomy, and composition.
